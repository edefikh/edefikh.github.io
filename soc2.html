🌿 SOC 2 Type 2 Compliance Plan for Small Startup (10 Employees)

📅 6-Month Timeline to Audit Readiness

Month 1: Planning & Scoping 🏗️

  • 🔹 Define audit scope (services, systems, locations).
  • 🔹 Choose a SOC 2 audit firm.
  • 🔹 Identify Trust Service Criteria (Security, Availability, etc.).
  • 🔹 Assign compliance roles & responsibilities.
  • 🔹 Set up a GRC tool (e.g., Vanta, Drata, Tugboat Logic).
  • 🔹 Configure Office 365 / Google Workspace for security logging.
  • 🔹 Draft security policies (Acceptable Use, Access Control, etc.).

Month 2: Risk Assessment & Controls 🔍

  • 🔹 Perform risk assessment and document risks.
  • 🔹 Implement access controls (SSO, MFA, Least Privilege).
  • 🔹 Deploy Security Awareness Training for employees.
  • 🔹 Implement an Incident Response Plan (test simulations).
  • 🔹 Set up logging & monitoring (Microsoft Defender, Google Admin Logs).
  • 🔹 Establish a vendor risk management process.

Month 3: Security Hardening & Compliance Policies 🔐

  • 🔹 Enforce strong password policies in Office 365 / Google Workspace.
  • 🔹 Configure device management & endpoint protection.
  • 🔹 Enable Data Loss Prevention (DLP) policies.
  • 🔹 Implement encryption for emails & stored data.
  • 🔹 Establish a Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery Plan.
  • 🔹 Conduct internal tabletop exercises for security incidents.

Month 4: Internal Audits & Testing ✅

  • 🔹 Perform internal audits using GRC software.
  • 🔹 Conduct vulnerability scans and penetration testing.
  • 🔹 Validate employee security training effectiveness.
  • 🔹 Audit access logs and privileged accounts.
  • 🔹 Ensure vendor compliance with security policies.
  • 🔹 Conduct a mock SOC 2 audit to identify gaps.

Month 5: Final Remediation & Audit Preparation 🚀

  • 🔹 Remediate gaps found in the internal audit.
  • 🔹 Verify compliance evidence collection is automated in GRC tools.
  • 🔹 Conduct a pre-audit readiness review with the auditor.
  • 🔹 Review and refine security documentation.
  • 🔹 Finalize incident response & business continuity drills.

Month 6: SOC 2 Type 2 Audit & Continuous Monitoring 🎯

  • 🔹 External auditors conduct the SOC 2 Type 2 audit.
  • 🔹 Address any findings from the audit.
  • 🔹 Implement a continuous compliance monitoring strategy.
  • 🔹 Automate monthly GRC reports & compliance checks.
  • 🔹 Plan for annual re-certification and policy updates.

🌱 Ongoing Monthly Compliance Tasks

  • ✅ Access Reviews – Ensure least privilege is enforced.
  • ✅ Security Awareness Training – Keep employees up to date.
  • ✅ Incident Response Testing – Conduct tabletop exercises.
  • ✅ Vendor Risk Management – Review third-party compliance.
  • ✅ System & Security Logs – Monitor for anomalies.

📌 GRC Tools & Office 365 / Google Workspace Integration

  • 🔹 Microsoft Purview Compliance Manager – for security & compliance monitoring.
  • 🔹 Google Workspace Security Center – for logging & alerts.
  • 🔹 Drata / Vanta / Tugboat Logic – for SOC 2 automation.
  • 🔹 Microsoft Intune / Google Endpoint Management – for device security.
🌿 By following this plan, your startup will be SOC 2 Type 2 ready in 6 months! 🚀
