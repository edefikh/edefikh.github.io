Introduction

Governance and compliance are critical components of any organization to ensure adherence to legal, regulatory, and ethical standards. This page provides an overview of frameworks, tools, and best practices.

Best Practices for Governance & Compliance

  • Conduct regular audits and assessments to identify risks and non-compliance.
  • Implement clear policies and procedures, and ensure employees are trained on them.
  • Use automation tools to streamline compliance reporting and monitoring.
  • Align your organization's compliance program with industry frameworks like NIST, ISO, or GDPR.
  • Document compliance efforts and maintain records for audits and regulatory requirements.
