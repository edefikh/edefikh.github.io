Mitigation Strategies

1. Firmware Management

  • Why It's Critical: Outdated firmware can introduce vulnerabilities and cause compatibility issues.
  • Schedule firmware updates quarterly and test before deployment.
  • Monitor vendor announcements for security advisories.
  • Use HP Smart Update Manager (SUM) for automated updates.

2. Redundancy Enhancements

  • Why It's Critical: Single points of failure (SPOFs) increase downtime risks.
  • Equip servers and storage with dual PSUs.
  • Configure RAID 6 or RAID 10 for data redundancy.
  • Enable multipathing for Fiber Channel (FC) connections.

3. Proactive Monitoring

  • Why It's Critical: Monitoring helps detect hardware issues before failures occur.
  • Use HP iLO for real-time server health monitoring.
  • Deploy SAN monitoring tools to track disk performance.
