Crypto Security Risks

Smart Contract Vulnerabilities

Smart contracts, once deployed, are immutable, making coding errors and vulnerabilities permanent risks. Exploits such as reentrancy attacks, integer overflows, and logic flaws can lead to the loss of funds.

Private Key Security

Private keys grant full control over crypto assets. Weak storage practices, phishing attacks, malware, and social engineering can lead to unauthorized access and fund theft.

Exchange Hacks

Cryptocurrency exchanges are prime targets for hackers. Poor security practices, insider threats, and vulnerabilities in centralized exchanges have led to significant losses of digital assets.
